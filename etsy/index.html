My Work At Etsy

This summer, I had the opportunity to work on the Listings & Inventory team at Etsy, part of Etsy's Seller Experience team. During my time there, I worked on inventory management tools for sellers on tablet, created research prototypes, and designed marketing materials for the launch of the Sell on Etsy iPad app.

My time at Etsy taught me a lot about designing business tools for creative entrepreneurs, and about wayfinding and native patterns in complex applications. Most of my work is pre-launch, so keep an eye out!
